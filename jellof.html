"Ladies and gentlemen, today we gather to discuss a matter of great importance. A debate that has played our society for far too long. A culinary conundrum that has left many of us scratching our heads... Jellof Rice vs Fried Rice!"


"On one hand, we have Jellof Rice- the spicy, sassy, and seductive queen of the rice world. She's the life of the party, always bringing the heat and leaving you wanting more."


"On the other hand, we have Fried Rice- the reliable, trustworthy, and slightly boring best friend. He''s always there for you, but lrt's be real, he's no jellof." But in all seriousness , cant't we just get along? Can't we just have both? I mean, who needs world peace when we can have Jellof and Fried Rice harmony?
